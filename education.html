Education Timeline

I went to Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram in New Delhi, India, where I studied the Sciences and Economics. My undergraduate years were spent at Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies in the same city, from where I graduated with an Honors in Business Administration and Marketing major. The first graduate school I went to was the Indian Institute of Mass Communication in New Delhi, India, to study Print Journalism.

In 2005, I moved to Singapore to pursue a Masters degree in Communication Studies (Research Track) from Nanyang Technological University, on a two-year graduate scholarship.

August of 2011 brought me to the University of Florida on a 4-year UF Graduate Fellowship at the College of Journalism and Communications, Division of Telecommunication.
